✦ What I'm all about ✦


Hi again! I’m Ivana, a first-year student at FCSE with a deep interest in both the technical and creative sides of development. I enjoy digging into new topics on my own, especially in areas like robotics and creative software development.

I’m passionate about solving problems through logic and creativity — I love puzzles, not just in games but in code and design. Whether it’s debugging a program or improving a user experience, I enjoy the challenge of making things work better and smarter.

Proficient in C++ with a solid foundation in structured and object-oriented programming, I’ve built a strong understanding of how things work behind the scenes. I’m also comfortable with front-end web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I’m currently learning and working in Unity, experimenting with both 2D and 3D game development. My goal is to create visually striking and meaningful games with deep, interactive stories that spark puzzle-solving thinking — the kind that makes people feel clever while having fun. I’m also taking a course in robotics, an area I’m genuinely excited about, aiming to build practical and helpful software that extends into the real world.

I’m the kind of person who doesn’t stop until things not only function, but feel right. I pay attention to detail, love learning in all forms, and I’m always full of ideas. Brainstorming is one of my strengths, and I thrive when I’m working on something that challenges both sides of my brain — the technical and the creative.

As a creative person, digital art is my outlet.
